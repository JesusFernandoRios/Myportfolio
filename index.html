Personal Bio

I am know for being a man of few words, so writing about myself like this doesn't come natural. For starters, I was born and raised in Nogales, Arizona. I was raised by my mother, Lourdes, and my beloved Nana. they raised me in a traditional Mexican household, where I learned the importance of never saying no to food, hard work and never holding back a big laugh. As I grew up I picked up a few of my own interests. I have a guitar with broken strings I haven't touched in forever. I have a number of hours I won't disclose spent on multiple video games, and I have even gotten into building Gaming PCs for myself and friends. I am always eager to try new things, and I am always looking towards the future.


Professional Bio

I've worked my way up in the Restaurant Industry, which lead me to become the Head Sushi Chef at a local Japanese restaurant. Although this has been fulfilling, I've always been drawn towards my hobbies involving computers. This has pushed me to decide to take a career change and start a new chapter in my life. I am currently enrolled at the University of Arizona's Programming Bootcamp. I am eager to refine and evolve my skill set and become more established as a business professional in this field. My goal after this bootcamp is to retire my Yanagiba knife and to dive deep into my new career as a Web Developer.
